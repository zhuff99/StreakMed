SECTION 6: SUGGESTED APP DESCRIPTION
----------------------------------------------------------------

Copy and paste this into the Description field, then edit as needed:

---

StreakMed helps you build a consistent medication habit by turning
your daily doses into a streak worth protecting.

ADD YOUR MEDICATIONS
Set up each medication with its name, dosage, schedule, and color.
Support for up to 4 doses per day, with individual reminders for
each one. Set it once and StreakMed handles the rest.

TRACK EVERY DOSE
Your Today tab shows every dose scheduled for the day. One tap to
mark it taken — the app cancels the reminder and logs the time
automatically.

BUILD YOUR STREAK
Every day you take all your doses on time counts toward your streak.
Watch it grow and stay motivated to keep the habit going.

SEE YOUR HISTORY
The calendar view gives you a color-coded month at a glance —
green for complete days, red for missed, yellow for partial. Tap
any day to see exactly what was taken and when.

SMART REMINDERS
Get notified at the right time for each dose. Set reminders up to
30 minutes early so you have time to prepare. Snooze or mark taken
right from the notification.

SCAN YOUR PRESCRIPTION (Optional)
Point your camera at a prescription label and let AI fill in the
medication details for you. Requires a free Claude API key.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
All your medication data is stored on your device. Nothing is ever
sent to a server or shared with third parties.

---


----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7: BUILD AND SUBMIT
----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] In Xcode, select "Any iOS Device (arm64)" as the build target
    (not a Simulator)

[ ] Go to Product > Archive
    This creates a release build. Takes a few minutes.

[ ] When the Organizer window opens, click "Distribute App"

[ ] Choose "App Store Connect" > Next

[ ] Choose "Upload" > Next

[ ] Leave all checkboxes at their defaults > Next

[ ] Sign in with your Apple ID if prompted

[ ] Click Upload and wait for it to complete (2-5 minutes)

[ ] Go back to appstoreconnect.apple.com
    Under your app > TestFlight or Builds, wait for the build
    to finish processing (usually 15-30 minutes)

[ ] Once processed, go to your App Store listing
    Scroll down to "Build" and select your uploaded build

[ ] Click "Add for Review" or "Submit for Review"

[ ] Answer the Export Compliance question:
      "Does your app use encryption?" — Select No
      (StreakMed does not implement custom encryption)

[ ] Confirm everything looks correct and Submit


----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8: AFTER SUBMISSION
----------------------------------------------------------------

[ ] Apple will review the app — typically 1-3 days for first submission
    You'll receive an email when approved or if there are issues

[ ] Common rejection reasons to watch for:
      - Missing privacy policy URL (already done)
      - Crash on launch (test on real device first)
      - Placeholder content in screenshots
      - App doesn't do what the description says

[ ] Once approved, the app goes live automatically unless you
    selected "Manually release this version" — in that case,
    go to App Store Connect and click Release

[ ] Share your App Store link with testers!
    Format: https://apps.apple.com/app/id[YOUR_APP_ID]
    Your App ID is visible in App Store Connect under App Information

FUTURE UPDATES (POST 1.0)

When you're ready to push an update:
